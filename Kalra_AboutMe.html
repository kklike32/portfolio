I am also an excellent team member. I have been in many team projects that help solve world problems. In 8th grade, I got together with my friends and decided to solve the water shortage issue in Dublin, CA. My group decided to target the public areas that use water specifically. We first proposed to add an area of turf in all the public parks so the city does not have to water it. We also proposed to use recycled water instead of fresh water to reuse water and limit the amount of it that our city uses. To decide on which idea was better, we surveyed people in our school and neighborhood to find which solution do they like better. The results we got showed us that over 80% of the people like the recycle water solution better. When we proposed this idea to the city, the council said that there are already laws in Dublin that require public areas to use recycled water. Even though the result did not turn out the way that we expected, we want to push this solutions into other cities that don't use recycled water.
