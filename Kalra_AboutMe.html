I am a freshman at Dublin High School. I am 14 years old and moved to the United States of America when I was 3. I was born in India and currently live in Dublin, California with my younger sister and parents.

I am highly motivated to secure a job in the computer science field. I believe I have the abilities and right support of teachers and parents to purse this career. Some of the abilities which help me are my leadership skills, team player,and ambitious learner.

I am currently the president of Math Tips and Tricks club at my school. Organizing the meetings every week to satisfy my club members is a challenging job because it requires lots of thinking and hard work to think of something new every time. Every Friday, I teach the members different tips and tricks on different math topics such as multiplication or volume. God gifted me with a logical brain and I use it to my advantage.

Another one of my talents is being an excellent team member. I have been in many team projects that help solve world problems. In 8th grade, I got together with my friends and decided to solve the water shortage issue in Dublin, CA. My group decided to target the public areas that use water specifically. We first proposed to add an area of turf in all the public parks so the city does not have to water it. We also proposed to use recycled water instead of fresh water to reuse water and limit the amount of it that our city uses. To decide on which idea was better, we surveyed people in our school and neighborhood to find which solution do they like better. The results we got showed us that over 80% of the people like the recycle water solution better. When we proposed this idea to the city, the council said that there are already laws in Dublin that require public areas to use recycled water. Even though the result did not turn out the way that we expected, we want to push this solutions into other cities that don't use recycled water.

Curiosity also drives me to go over and beyond what I learn at school. I do my research at home for stuff that I want to get better at in my own time. Being curious and open minded has always helped me in solving challenges.
